SelektaDx™: Breaking barriers in real-time PCR technology

The SelektaDx™ is a versatile diagnostic platform, easily customizable for detecting multiple DNA, RNA, or combined DNA/RNA targets in a single test. Building on our proven expertise in molecular diagnostics and leveraging modified intercepting and blocker PCR chemistry, we have developed a streamlined two-step real-time PCR assay that delivers clear, multiplexed results within 90 minutes on any standard real-time PCR instrument.

This modified intercepting multiplex real-time PCR assay enables the simultaneous detection of wild-type and resistance-associated mutations across diverse pathogens. Its dual-layer detection system employs MutaSelect™ Primers to suppress wild-type sequences and FluoroSelect™ Probes to accurately detect resistance-associated mutations. The assay can be scaled to identify dozens of clinically relevant targets directly from complex samples, making it suitable for highly multiplexed testing of antimicrobial resistance (AMR) and zoonotic infections, and deployable even in resource-limited diagnostic settings.

As can be seen below, the SelektaDxTM real-time PCR platform is designed to enable the simultaneous detection of both wild-type and resistance-associated mutations across complex disease regimens, including tuberculosis, HIV/AIDS, and cancer. The platform can identify up to 30 clinically relevant targets (a combination of wild-types and mutations) in a single run, delivering >97% analytical sensitivity and >98% specificity. Results are generated in just 90 minutes using standard real-time PCR equipment, with a hands-on time of less than 15 minutes. The assay requires less than 200 µL of raw clinical sample, works directly from sputum, blood, or biopsy material, and has been optimized to function in resource-limited diagnostic settings, with minimal infrastructure requirements. Its dual-layer detection system reduces false negatives by >90% compared to conventional PCR, while retaining affordability and scalability for high-throughput or decentralized testing.

Applications of SelektaDx™ diagnostic platform:

The SelektaDx™ platform is designed as a highly multiplexed, two-step real-time PCR system that delivers rapid and reliable detection across diverse health domains. Its customizable chemistry enables simultaneous identification of wild-type and resistance-associated mutations, making it uniquely versatile.

01

Veterinary & Zoonotic Infections: Rapid identification of helminthic parasites in livestock and emerging zoonotic pathogens affecting One Health ecosystems.

02

Oncology in Humans & Animals: Early detection of genetic mutations linked to cancers in humans, zoonotic reservoirs, and companion animals.

03

Human Health & Antimicrobial Resistance: Detection of multidrug resistance in tuberculosis, HIV, HCV, and HPV to guide timely treatment decisions.

04

Public Health Surveillance: Scalable deployment for decentralized testing, supporting national programs, outbreak control, and AMR monitoring in low-resource settings.

05

Research & Translational Medicine: Customizable assay panels for exploratory studies, resistance surveillance, and biomarker validation.
