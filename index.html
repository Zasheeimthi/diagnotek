Founded by visionary scientists and diagnostics innovators with over 50 years of combined expertise, Mitti Lifetek Diagnostics is a research-driven biotechnology company redefining global healthcare solutions. Our multidisciplinary team is committed to transforming the detection, prevention, and management of zoonotic infections, antimicrobial resistance, and cancers in both humans and companion animals.

Through proprietary molecular, immunological, and reagent platforms, we deliver rapid, affordable, and scalable diagnostics that provide point-of-care confirmatory results — empowering clinicians with unmatched confidence and precision.

Aligned with the WHO One Health Joint Plan of Action, global AMR strategies, and international commissions on zoonotic disease control, our portfolio strengthens sustainable health systems and accelerates progress toward the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

We operate with a strong commitment to global quality and regulatory standards, including ISO 9001 and ISO 13485. By uniting science and innovation, we deliver rapid, reliable solutions that protect humans, animals, and the environment — advancing the One Health mission and shaping a healthier world across communities and continents.

Technology

Science you trust. Solutions that last,
built for today and tomorrow.

Our technology portfolio unites three breakthrough platforms — RAR-ELISA™ for ultra-sensitive serology, SelektaDx™ for rapid multiplex molecular diagnostics, and ApiOne™/ApiTwo™ ATP Slayers for next-generation hygiene reagents — delivering scalable, affordable, and field-ready solutions across human, veterinary, and One Health applications.

RAR-ELISA™

Redefining Rapid Serology

RAR-ELISA™ is an innovative serological platform for ultra-sensitive antibody detection using lipid-modified recombinant antigens. Delivering results in just 90 minutes with a 15-minute coating step, it is portable, field-ready, and enables reliable human, veterinary, and zoonotic infection testing — strengthening One Health surveillance.

Read more

SelektaDx™

Breaking barriers in real-time PCR technology

The SelektaDx™ is a versatile diagnostic platform, easily customizable for detecting multiple DNA, RNA, or combined DNA/RNA targets in a single test. Building on our proven expertise in molecular diagnostics and leveraging modified intercepting and blocker PCR chemistry, we have developed a streamlined two-step real-time PCR assay that delivers clear, multiplexed results within 90 minutes on any standard real-time PCR instrument.

Read more

ApiOne™ ATP Slayers:

Redefining ATP-based bioluminescence technology

The recombinant Shigella flexneri apyrase is a patented enzyme that hydrolyzes extracellular ATP, ADP, and their complexes into AMP and inorganic phosphate within seconds. Unlike conventional apyrases, it is engineered for high stability, rapid action, and scalable production, making it uniquely suited for advanced biotech applications.
