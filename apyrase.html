ApiOne™ ATP Slayers: Redefining ATP-based bioluminescence technology

The recombinant Shigella flexneri apyrase is a patented enzyme that hydrolyzes extracellular ATP, ADP, and their complexes into AMP and inorganic phosphate within seconds. Unlike conventional apyrases, it is engineered for high stability, rapid action, and scalable production, making it uniquely suited for advanced biotech applications.

The enzyme is available in two optimized variants:

01

ApiOne™:

tailored for high-throughput diagnostics, PCR cleanup, sequencing workflows, and bioluminescence assays.

02

ApiTwo™:

designed for therapeutic, plasma, and biosensor applications, where ADP hydrolysis plays a critical role in reducing platelet aggregation and enhancing assay sensitivity.
