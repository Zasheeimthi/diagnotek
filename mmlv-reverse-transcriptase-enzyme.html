MMLV Reverse Transcriptase

MMLV (Moloney Murine Leukaemia Virus) Reverse Transcriptase is a high-performance enzyme used to transcribe RNA into complementary DNA (cDNA). This enzyme is pivotal in a variety of molecular biology applications like cDNA library construction, RT-PCR, and RNA sequencing.
