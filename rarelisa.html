RAR-ELISA™: Redefining Rapid Serology

RAR-ELISA™ (Rapid, Affordable, Reliable ELISA) is an innovative serological platform for ultra-sensitive antibody detection using lipid-modified recombinant antigens. Delivering results in just 90 minutes with a 15-minute coating step, it is portable, field-ready, and enables reliable human, veterinary, and zoonotic infection testing — strengthening One Health surveillance.

The RAR-ELISA™ platform achieves breakthrough performance through three key innovations:

Applications of RAR-ELISA™

Together, these innovations reduce both cost and turnaround time while preserving the ultrasensitivity and accuracy required for reliable disease detection. The platform is designed for field deployment, where tests can be conducted by semi-skilled workers using a portable reader, eliminating the need for specialized laboratories. Its flexibility enables decentralized evaluations across human health, veterinary medicine, and zoonotic infections, covering neglected tropical diseases, parasitic infections, and antimicrobial resistance surveillance. Beyond clinical diagnostics, RAR-ELISA™ can be applied in public health screening programs, livestock health management, food safety monitoring, and environmental surveillance, making it a truly versatile tool. By enabling early intervention, scalable surveillance, and integration into One Health programs, the platform strengthens global capacity to detect, control, and prevent emerging and re-emerging infectious threats.

01

Lipid modification for enhanced sensitivity:

Most rapid tests suffer from low sensitivity and false negatives, delaying diagnosis and treatment. RAR-ELISA™ overcomes this by leveraging site-specific bacterial lipidation, attaching an N-acyl-S-diacylglyceryl moiety to the N-terminal cysteine of target proteins or peptides. This improves antigen/antibody orientation and hydrophobic binding to ELISA plates, enabling ultrasensitive detection of very low antibody titres.

02

Proprietary Xact InstaCoat™ for rapid coating:

Our patented InstaCoat™ buffer enables antigen or antibody coating on microtiter plates in just 10 minutes at room temperature (vs. 12–24 hours with conventional methods), dramatically reducing assay turnaround without compromising sensitivity.

03

Trade tweaks for cost reduction:

By combining an affordable fluorescence substrate with proprietary assay know-how, RAR-ELISA™ delivers ultra-high sensitivity for low-titre antibody detection, ensuring accuracy even in early infections. Crucially, it eliminates the need for costly surface-charged ELISA plates.

As shown below, our proprietary lipid modification of antigens enhances detection sensitivity when coated on ELISA plates and probed with hyper-immune mouse antisera: (A) native vs. lipid-modified CL1, (B) native vs. lipid-modified Ras domain.

When the serum samples of 350 livestock animals were tested, the RAR-ELISA™ platform demonstrated superior performance with lipid-modified recombinant antigens. Detection sensitivity increased to 97.8% for Fascioliasis and 95.7% for Haemonchosis, compared with 81.7% and 83.8% of ELISA available in the market, using native antigens. This robust improvement confirms the platform’s potential for early and reliable diagnosis of these neglected parasitic diseases.
